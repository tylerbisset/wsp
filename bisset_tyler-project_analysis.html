Client: Christ Episcopal Church

Website: www.christchurchseattle.org

Designer: Tyler Bisset

Site Intent

The website for the Christ Episcopal Church has a number of areas that require a re-design. The purpose of this redesign is to reorganize the content of the CEC into 6 web pages. These pages will be more concise and will help increase internet traffic to the site. This will in turn help raise awareness of the church in the Seattle area and will increase visitors and members.

Currently the CEC site does not validate according to the W3C validator test. There are currently 52 HTML errors. In addition, the code used on the site is out dated and needs to be brought up to the current web standards. The CEC website does not use an external CSS style sheet which is a big contributing factor to the validation errors and overall appearance of the site. For this re-design, an external style sheet will be applied and the tables and other dividers will be removed to help clean up the appearance.

The layout and structure of the site also needs to be re-designed. Currently there are two different navigation areas in columns on either side of the site. These navigation areas are confusing and not very clear or different than the main call to action. This re-design will remove the two navigations and add a horizontal navigation bar at the top of the page which will incorporate the 6 pages. By doing this, more space will be created for the main CTA area which will contain content like news items.

Finally the CEC website needs analytic data. Currently Alexa.com has very little information about the CEC which is hurting business. This re-design will help implement keywords and search engine optimization to increase traffic.

Competitive Analysis

The CEC is a Christian, historic, community driven organization. Their mission is to be faithful to their mission as a parish, to know Christ, and to make Christ known through prayer, liturgy, outreach, and Christian community.

Keywords:

Christian churches in Seattle, Seattle Christ church, Christ Episcopal Church, Seattle Churches, Christ Church, Christian churches, Episcopal Church

Competitors:

These are the top ranking competitors to the CEC according to the result of keywords using Google search:

  • Mars Hill Church
  • Greenwood Christian Church
  • Seattle Church of Christ
  • West Seattle Christian Church

The main competitor for the CEC is the Mars Hill Church. With an Alexa traffic rank of 80,703 globally and 23,399 in the US they dominate the church scene in Seattle. They receive roughly 20% of their traffic through search engines and also have a whopping 1,009 sites linking in. These statistics show the importance of optimizing the CEC website for search engines and using correct keywords to pull in more traffic.

Targeted Audience

Based upon initial research the target audience for the CEC website are males and females, ages 25-44. These persons have children and have some college to a college education. The following are two user profiles describing different scenarios.

Male User Profile:

Because of his busy schedule Jason likes to check his calendar at work frequently. He typically only uses the web for 30 minutes or less and likes to find information fast. He is looking for a focused Church that abides by a strict schedule so that he can fit his faith into his busy lifestyle.
